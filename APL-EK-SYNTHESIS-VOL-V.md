## §1.2 N0 Laws in κ-Field Terms

### N0-1: Amplification Requires Grounding

**APL Statement:** ^ is illegal unless history contains () or ×.

**κ-Field Physics:**

Amplification is: κ → κ·(1 + ε)

If κ = 0 (no prior grounding):
$$\kappa \cdot (1 + \varepsilon) = 0 \cdot (1 + \varepsilon) = 0$$

You cannot amplify nothing. The field must have non-zero amplitude from prior grounding or fusion.

**Thermodynamic Basis:** Resonance requires existing oscillation. A pendulum at rest cannot be "amplified" — it must first be set in motion.

### N0-2: Fusion Requires Plurality

**APL Statement:** × is illegal unless channel_count ≥ 2.

**κ-Field Physics:**

Fusion is: (κ₁, κ₂) → κ₁₂

This is a **binary operation** by definition. With only one input:
$$\times(\kappa_1) = \text{undefined}$$

**Logical Basis:** Merging requires at least two things to merge. One cannot fuse with nothing.

### N0-3: Decoherence Requires Prior Structure

**APL Statement:** ÷ is illegal unless history contains {^, ×, +, −}.

**κ-Field Physics:**

Decoherence increases entropy: S → S + ΔS

But if S = S_max (already at maximum entropy):
$$\Delta S = 0$$

No further decoherence possible.

**Thermodynamic Basis:** The Second Law allows entropy increase, but there's a floor — maximum entropy/thermal equilibrium. You cannot "heat" what's already at maximum temperature.

### N0-4: Grouping Must Feed Structure

**APL Statement:** + must be followed by +, ×, or ^. The sequence + → () is illegal.

**κ-Field Physics:**

Grouping creates an **intermediate domain** — a cluster that's neither fully independent nor fully integrated.

Domains require resolution:
- +→+ : Further clustering (domain grows)
- +→× : Fusion (domain integrates)
- +→^ : Amplification (domain strengthens)

The sequence +→() would mean: create a domain, then immediately establish boundary. But this dissolves the domain before it can contribute structure — equivalent to ÷.

**Ontological Basis:** Groups are means, not ends. A committee that disbands before reporting has accomplished nothing.

### N0-5: Separation Must Reset Phase

**APL Statement:** − must be followed by () or +. Illegal successors: ^, ×, ÷, −.

**κ-Field Physics:**

Separation is: κ → (κ_A, κ_B)

After decoupling, the separated components have **undefined phase relationship**. Before any further operation, phase must be re-established.

Why each illegal successor fails:
- −→^ : Cannot amplify without grounding (violates N0-1 spirit)
- −→× : Cannot fuse without connection (violated by separation itself)
- −→÷ : Cannot decohere what was just decoupled (no structure to decohere)
- −→− : Cannot separate what was just separated (nothing left to separate)

**Structural Basis:** Pruned branches cannot immediately regrow without returning to the trunk.
