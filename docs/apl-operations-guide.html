Phi (Structure Field) Symbol: Phi

Controls geometry, stability, and boundaries. The structural scaffold of reality.

  • Affinity: M (Modulation), C (Collapse)
  • Domain: Spatial organization, crystalline forms, topological constraints
  • Chemical Role: Molecular geometry, bonding topology
  • Neural Role: Structural plasticity, synaptic architecture
e (Energy Field) Symbol: e (Euler)

Controls dynamics, oscillations, and flow. The engine of transformation.

  • Affinity: U (Up), E (Expansion)
  • Domain: Kinetic processes, thermodynamics, activation energy
  • Chemical Role: Reaction energetics, electron transfer
  • Neural Role: Action potentials, metabolic activity
Pi (Emergence Field) Symbol: Pi

Controls selection, information, and complexity. The arbiter of emergence.

  • Affinity: D (Down), Mod (Modulation)
  • Domain: Information processing, pattern selection, entropy management
  • Chemical Role: Molecular weight, phase transitions
  • Neural Role: Decision making, pattern recognition
